How it started

I first got interested in animation from watching YouTube Shorts - those quick animated skits that grab you in three seconds. I started noticing things I'd never paid attention to before: the timing of a joke, the way a pause makes a punchline land, how exaggerated gestures make characters feel alive, and how camera framing changes the entire mood of a scene.

That curiosity turned into this project. Over 17 weeks, I taught myself Blender and created a short dance animation from scratch. This portfolio documents every stumble, breakthrough, and lesson along the way.
