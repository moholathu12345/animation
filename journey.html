Week 1–2

Software installation and interface familiarization

SetupUIWorkflow

Set up Blender and learned the basics so I could start animating instead of just exploring.

Anchor

What I tried

Navigation, timeline controls, keyframes, and quick playblasts to review motion properly.

What went wrong

The interface felt overwhelming at first and I kept jumping between tools without understanding the workflow.

What I changed

I committed to a simple loop: pose, keyframe, playback, adjust. That made Blender feel manageable.

What I learned

Once the workflow is clear, progress becomes consistent.
